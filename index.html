üé§ Effective Interview Strategies

Before the Interview

  • Actually use the company's product/service
  • Check recent news and funding announcements
  • Analyze competitors and market trends
  • Prepare a question list (15-20 questions)

During the Interview

  • Create a comfortable atmosphere with ice-breaking
  • Start with open-ended questions
  • Request specific examples and numbers
  • Flexibly respond to unexpected answers

After the Interview

  • Send a thank-you email within 24 hours
  • Organize recordings/notes and extract key points
  • Prepare additional questions and follow-up
  • Begin writing the article draft

‚öñÔ∏è Journalism Ethics & Guidelines

Essential Guidelines

  • Accuracy All information must be fact-checked before publication
  • Fairness Maintain objective perspective without bias toward specific companies
  • Transparency Disclose any conflicts of interest
  • Confidentiality Never publish off-the-record content

Common Pitfalls to Avoid

  • Avoid writing promotional articles
  • Refrain from competitor bashing or unfounded criticism
  • Don't use copyrighted images/materials without permission
  • Don't distort or exaggerate interviewees' statements
